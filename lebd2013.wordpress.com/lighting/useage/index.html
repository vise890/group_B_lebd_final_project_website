Usage

Usage of artificial lighting was assessed based on consideration when occupants leave the house for education or work and when they come back. Bedrooms were assumed to be study areas. Therefore, they are used during the afternoon to do work at home. Information obtained from DiaLUX evo and DiaLUX 4.11 were put together so usage in a typical summer and a typical winter day could be assessed.

Monthly demand graphs were plotted for each room using Excel spreadsheet.
